The pillars of the family include the following;

  1. Love. Is the origin of our existence and started with our creator, the Almighty God. As a family we ought to embrace love in all we do. For example, love should define how we relate with our spouses, friends and even strangers.

  2. Source. God is our source. As a result of this, we should remember where we come from and the nature of our source.

  3. Identity. This is our association as a family. We need to understand who we are and claim our values according to who we are. Hence families should explore their identity in order to keep focusing in the right things.

  4. Purpose. This is the driving force for any family. There has to exist the real reason for the family existence. Where there is purpose, the bond in the family remains to be strong.

  5. Potential. This imply the level of our exploit. As a family, we are destined for greatness and no one should limit us from achieving what God planned in our lives.

  6. Destiny. Where are we going as a family? Our choices as family should be wise in order to achieve the goals set for us within a given period. Destiny shapes our interactions and how we do things in life.

  7. Unity. For the family to move forward, there is need for working together. And this results to harmony which brings unity.
